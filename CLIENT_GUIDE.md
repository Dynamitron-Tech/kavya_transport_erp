# Kavya Transport ERP — User Guide

## What This System Does
Kavya Transport ERP helps your transport business run daily work in one place. You can add clients, create jobs, make LR and e-way bill records, assign vehicles and drivers, track trips, record expenses, create invoices, collect payments, and see business reports. This means your team does not need separate books, files, and apps for each task.

---

## How To Login

1. Open the system link in your browser.
2. You will see the login page.
3. Enter your email.
4. Enter your password.
5. Click the Login button.
6. You will go to your dashboard.

### Screenshot Description
- **Screen 1: Login Page**
  - Shows two boxes: Email and Password.
  - Shows one button: Login.
- **Screen 2: Dashboard**
  - Shows menu on the left.
  - Shows cards with important numbers (jobs, trips, payments, etc.).

### If login fails
1. Check email spelling.
2. Check password spelling (capital/small letters).
3. Ask Admin to reset your password.

## Guide For Each Role

## Admin Guide

### What Admin can do
- Manage all users and all business data.
- Add and update clients, vehicles, drivers, routes.
- Review jobs, trips, LR, and documents.
- View full finance and reports.
- Monitor system health and access.

### Admin steps (daily)
1. Login as Admin.
2. Open Dashboard and check alerts.
3. Open Clients, Vehicles, and Drivers pages and verify new entries.
4. Open Jobs and Trips pages to check active work.
5. Open Reports to review today or weekly business status.
6. Open Settings if policy or company data needs update.

---

## Manager Guide

### What Manager can do
- Create jobs for clients.
- Plan and assign work.
- Track ongoing trips.
- Follow delivery status and completion.

### How Manager works
1. Open **Jobs / Orders**.
2. Click **Create Job**.
3. Enter pickup and delivery details.
4. Save the job.
5. Open the job and assign a vehicle and driver.
6. Open **Trips** and check progress (started, reached, closed).
7. Open **Tracking** to see vehicle movement.
8. Close the process after delivery confirmation.

---

## Fleet Manager Guide

### What Fleet Manager can do
- Manage vehicle condition and availability.
- Manage fuel records.
- Manage tyre records.
- Manage service and maintenance schedules.
- Watch alerts for expiry and maintenance.

### Fleet Manager steps
1. Open **Fleet Dashboard**.
2. Check vehicle availability and alerts.
3. Open **Fleet Vehicles** and update vehicle status if needed.
4. Open **Fuel** and add daily fuel entries.
5. Open **Tyres** and update tyre activity.
6. Open **Maintenance** and create service records.
7. Open **Fleet Reports** to review cost and performance.

---

## Accountant Guide

### What Accountant can do
- Create invoices.
- Record incoming and outgoing payments.
- Check ledger.
- Track receivables and payables.
- View finance reports.

### Accountant steps
1. Open **Accountant Dashboard**.
2. Open **Invoices** and click create.
3. Select client and trip/job details.
4. Save the invoice.
5. Open **Payments** and record payment when received.
6. Open **Receivables** to see pending collections.
7. Open **Payables** to see pending outgoing payments.
8. Open **Ledger** and verify entries.
9. Open **Reports** for profit, expense, and monthly summary.

---

## Project Associate Guide

### What Project Associate can do
- Create and manage LR.
- Create and manage e-way bill records.
- Support trip updates and closure.
- Maintain document flow.

### Project Associate steps
1. Open **Lorry Receipts**.
2. Click **Create LR**.
3. Fill consignor, consignee, origin, destination, and goods details.
4. Save LR.
5. Open **E-way Bill** page and create record linked to LR.
6. Open **Trips** and update status as transport moves.
7. After delivery, mark trip complete/closed as per process.
8. Upload required documents if needed.

---

## Driver Guide

### What Driver can do
- View assigned trips.
- Mark attendance/check-in.
- Start and update trip progress.
- Submit trip expenses.
- View own documents.

### Driver steps
1. Login with Driver account.
2. Open **My Trips** and check assigned trip.
3. Open **Attendance** and mark check-in.
4. Start trip from trip page when journey begins.
5. Update trip status when reaching destination.
6. Open **Expenses** and submit fuel/toll/other expenses.
7. Open **My Documents** to view required papers.

---

## Daily Workflow

### One shipment from start to payment
1. Manager creates a job for client transport need.
2. Project Associate creates LR for that job.
3. Project Associate creates e-way bill record.
4. Manager/Fleet team assigns vehicle and driver.
5. Driver starts trip and updates progress.
6. Fleet/Manager tracks vehicle on live map.
7. Driver submits expenses during/after trip.
8. Trip is marked closed after delivery.
9. Accountant creates invoice for completed work.
10. Accountant records payment when money is received.
11. Accountant checks outstanding list for unpaid invoices.

## Common Tasks

## How to add a new client
1. Open **Clients**.
2. Click **Add Client**.
3. Enter client name, phone, email, city, and state.
4. Click **Save**.

## How to add a new vehicle
1. Open **Vehicles**.
2. Click **Add Vehicle**.
3. Enter registration number and vehicle details.
4. Click **Save**.

## How to create a job
1. Open **Jobs / Orders**.
2. Click **Create Job**.
3. Enter pickup, delivery, and load details.
4. Select client.
5. Click **Save**.

## How to generate an invoice
1. Open **Invoices**.
2. Click **Create Invoice**.
3. Select client and completed trip/job.
4. Enter amount details.
5. Click **Save**.

## How to check outstanding payments
1. Open **Receivables**.
2. Filter by date or client (optional).
3. View invoices marked unpaid/partly paid.

## How to see live vehicle locations
1. Open **Tracking** or **Fleet Tracking**.
2. Select active trip or vehicle.
3. View location and movement on map.

## Troubleshooting

## Problem: Cannot login
1. Check email/password.
2. Check if Caps Lock is on.
3. Ask Admin to reset your password.

## Problem: Page not loading data
1. Refresh the page.
2. Check internet connection.
3. Logout and login again.
4. Inform support if issue continues.

## Problem: Button is disabled
1. Check if all required fields are filled.
2. Check if your role has access for that action.
3. Ask Admin if permission update is needed.

## Problem: Trip not visible
1. Confirm trip was assigned to correct driver/vehicle.
2. Check date/status filters.
3. Ask Manager to verify assignment.

## Problem: Invoice not appearing
1. Check if trip is completed/closed.
2. Refresh Invoices page.
3. Check date filter.
4. Contact Accountant/Admin.

## Support

For help, contact your internal support team:

1. **First level:** Branch/Admin user (for login, access, basic usage).
2. **Second level:** Operations lead (for job, trip, LR, assignment process).
3. **Third level:** Technical support team (for system errors, page issues, data mismatch).

When reporting an issue, share:
1. Your name and role.
2. Page name.
3. What you were trying to do.
4. Error message (if shown).
5. Time when issue happened.
